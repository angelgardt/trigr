Triangles

Symbols

\(\triangle ABC\) a triangle
\(A\), \(B\), \(C\) the vertices of the triangle
\((A_x, A_y)\), \((B_x, B_y)\), \((C_x, C_y)\) the coordinates of vertices of the triangle
\(AB = c\), \(BC = a\), \(AC = b\) the edges of the triangle
\(\angle A = \alpha\), \(\angle B = \beta\), \(\angle C = \gamma\) the internal angles of the triangle
\(P\) the perimeter of the triangle
\(s\) the semiperimeter of the triangle
\(A_\triangle\) the area of the triangle
\(h_a\), \(h_b\), \(h_c\) the altitudes of the triangle
\(a\), \(b\) and \(c\) the bases of \(h_a\), \(h_b\), and \(h_c\) respectively
\(H_A\), \(H_B\), \(H_C\) the feet of \(h_a\), \(h_b\) and \(h_c\) respectively
\((H_{Ax}, H_{Ay})\), \((H_{Bx}, H_{By})\), \((H_{Cx}, H_{Cy})\) the coordinates of \(H_A\), \(H_B\), \(H_C\) respectively
\(H\) the orthocenter of the triangle
\((H_x, H_y)\) the cordinates of the orthocenter
\(m_a\), \(m_b\), \(m_c\) the medians of a triangle
\(M_A\), \(M_B\), \(M_C\) the midpoints of \(a\), \(b\) and \(c\) respectively
\((M_{Ax}, M_{Ay})\), \((M_{Bx}, M_{By})\), \((M_{Cx}, M_{Cy})\) the coordinates of \(M_A\), \(M_B\) and \(M_C\) respectively
\(G\) the centroid of the triangle
\(l_a\), \(l_b\), \(l_c\) the internal angle bisectors
\(L_a\), \(L_b\), \(L_c\) the feet of angle bisectors
\((L_{Ax}, L_{Ay})\), \((L_{Bx}, L_{By})\), \((L_{Cx}, L_{Cy})\) the coordinates of \(L_a\), \(L_b\) and \(L_c\) respectively
\(I\) the incenter of the triangle
\(r\) the inradius, radius of the incircle
\(L_I\) the circumference of incircle
\(A_I\) the area of incircle
\(I_a\), \(I_b\), \(I_c\) the touchpoints of the incircle of the triangle
\((I_{ax}, I_{ay})\), \((I_{bx}, I_{by})\), \((I_{cx}, I_{cy})\) the coordinates of \(I_a\), \(I_b\), \(I_c\)
\(J_A\), \(J_B\), \(J_C\) the excenters of the triangle
\((J_{Ax}, J_{Ay})\), \((J_{Bx}, J_{By})\), \((J_{Cx}, J_{Cy})\) the coordinates of \(J_A\), \(J_B\), \(J_C\) respectively
\(r_A\), \(r_B\), \(r_C\) the radii of the excircles
\(T_A\), \(T_B\), \(T_C\) the touchpoints of the excircles
\((T_{Ax}, T_{Ay})\), \((T_{Ax}, T_{Ay})\), \((T_{Ax}, T_{Ay})\) the coordinates of \(T_A\), \(T_B\), \(T_C\) respectively
\(N\) Nagel point
\((N_x, N_y)\) the coordinates of Nagel point
\(p_a\), \(p_b\), \(p_c\) perpendicular bisectors
\(O\) the circumcenter of the triangle
\((O_x, O_y)\) the coordinates of circumcenter
\(R\) the radius of circumcircle
\(L_O\) the circumference of circumcircle
\(A_O\) the area of circumcircle

Law of cosines

\[ a^2 = b^2 + c^2 - 2bc \cos \alpha \]

\[ b^2 = a^2 + c^2 - 2ac \cos \beta \]

\[ c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2ab \cos \gamma \]

Law of sines

\[ \frac{a}{\sin \alpha} = \frac{b}{\sin \beta} = \frac{c}{\sin \gamma} = 2R \]

\[ \frac{\sin \alpha}{a} = \frac{\sin \beta}{b} = \frac{\sin \gamma}{c} \]

Law of tangents

\[ \frac{a-b}{a+b} = \frac{\tan \dfrac{\alpha - \beta}{2}}{\tan \dfrac{\alpha + \beta}{2}} \]

Law of cotangents

\[ \cot \frac{\alpha}{2} = \frac{s-a}{2} \]

\[ \cot \frac{\beta}{2} = \frac{s-b}{2} \]

\[ \cot \frac{\gamma}{2} = \frac{s-c}{2} \]

\[ \frac{p-a}{\cot \dfrac{\alpha}{2}} = \frac{p-b}{\cot \dfrac{\beta}{2}} = \frac{p-c}{\cot \dfrac{\gamma}{2}} = r \]

Projection Formulae

\[ a = b \cos \gamma + c \cos \beta \]

\[ b = c \cos \alpha + a \cos \gamma \]

\[ c = a \cos \beta + b \cos \alpha \]

Mollweide’s formula

\[ \frac{a+b}{c} = \frac{\cos \dfrac{\alpha - \beta}{2}}{\sin \dfrac{\gamma}{2}} = \frac{\cos \dfrac{\alpha - \beta}{2}}{\cos \dfrac{\alpha + \beta}{2}} \]

\[ \frac{a-b}{c} = \frac{\sin \dfrac{\alpha - \beta}{2}}{\cos \dfrac{\gamma}{2}} = \frac{\sin \dfrac{\alpha - \beta}{2}}{\sin \dfrac{\alpha + \beta}{2}} \]
